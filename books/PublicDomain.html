About Public Domain Books

The intellectual property (IP) rights have expired for books and stories that are in the Public Domain, or the author has decided to put their work in the public domain. This means they can be freely copied, repackaged, redistributed, and resold. Popular Public Domain books have been made into ebooks of various qualities, from excellent, with introductions and notes, while others are plain, or incomplete.

Public Domain stories are readily available on internet sites for free at like Gutenberg.org. eBook stores sell Public Domain books, example: The Legend of Sleepy Hollow for 99 cents. The value add with this book, is the front cover and illustrations. However, some customer reviews mention that text was missing.
