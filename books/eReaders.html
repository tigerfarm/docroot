eBook Readers

1. eBook reader for the Firefox browser.

To read EPUB files from Firefox, install the epubreader (free) plugin. To install the plugin, click the link, epubreader, and then click + Add to Firefox. The plugin will be installed.

After the epubreader plugin has been installed, click [EPUB] to view a sample, The Wonderful Wizard of Oz (259KB).

2. Apple iPhone and iPad eReader applications

For iPhone and iPad devices. EPUB files are loaded into iTunes on the computer, then synched to the device. On the device, use the iBook application to open the book and read it.

3. There are eReader programs for computers (Windows, MAC).

For MAC and Windows, Adobe has an EPUB ereader: Adobe Digital Editions Home (free).

Example, Kindle ereader program to read MOBI ebooks on various types of computers, examples: Windows PC and MAC.

4. Full color Android based tablets by eBook Sellers with their ereader programs pre-installed.

5. Full color tablets: Apple iPhone and iPad, and Android based tablets by technology companies,

Android examples, Google's Nexus, Samsung's Galaxy Tab

6. Black and white e-ink readers: most require a read light. Good for reading in sun light.
