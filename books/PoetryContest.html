2017 Keats-Shelley Poetry Contest

Link to contest description and rules.
Submissions open: 1st September 2016 Deadline for entries: 15th January 2017  .
Entries may be submitted via admin@ksprize.onmicrosoft.com. There is no charge for entering. Include a list of your entries, your name, address, and full contact details in your email.
The theme 'To a Friend,' commemorating the 200th anniversary of the publication of John Keats's first poetry anthology.
Poems should not be in a style that imitates another work, artist, or period.
Poems are not to exceed 30 lines.
Essays can focus on any aspect of the work and lives of Keats, Shelley, and their circles, and should not exceed 3,000 words.
All shortlisted candidates will enjoy seeing their work published online or in print, and will be invited to celebrate with us at our annual Awards Ceremony, which takes place in the second half of April at the Royal Festival Hall, in London. Winners will share £4,000 in prize money.
